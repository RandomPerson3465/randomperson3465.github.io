    Livecountsedit was created with a Discord user called "Sync" sent a DM to RandomPerson3465 about editing Livecounts.net subscriber counts. Livecountsedit 1.0 used a copy of the Livecounts.net website design. The idea for fake subscriber counts goes back to PewDiePie vs. T-Series, during which RandomPerson3465 uploaded a video of T-Series "losing" subscribers on a modified SocialBlade live subscriber count on 22 January 2019, and an April Fools video of T-Series "losing" subscribers due to an audit on Akshatmittal live sub count. Both of these videos have been deleted.
