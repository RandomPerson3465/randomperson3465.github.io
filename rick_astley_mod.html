Rick Astley Mod

The Rick Astley Mod adds rickrolling and various rickroll items to Minecraft (such as rickroll tools and armor, animated rickroll items, and rickroll QR code items).

This is my first Minecraft mod, and I made it as a joke.

This mod is for Minecraft Forge 1.12.2.
